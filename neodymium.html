  • CHARACTERISTICS: Neodymium has a shiny, silvery appearance, but being one of the most reactive lanthanides, it oxidizes quickly in air. Neodymium is one of the rare chemical elements that can be found in homes in devices such as color TVs, fluorescent lamps, and more. It is the second most abundant rare earth element in nature and is almost as abundant as copper.
  • TECHNOLOGICAL APPLICATIONS: In technology, neodymium magnets are essential. They are found in hard drives, speakers, headphones, and microphones, where their ability to generate a powerful magnetic field in a small space is fundamental to the operation of these devices.
