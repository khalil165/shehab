Characteristics and Applications

  • Characteristics: Samarium is a rare earth metal, mainly used in magnetic alloys, nuclear applications, and geological dating.
  • Technological applications: permanent magnets (SmCo), nuclear reactors, catalysts, fiber optics, and lasers.
  • Main deposit locations: China, United States, Australia, Russia, and India.

Impact on Health and Environment

  • Health effects: Samarium can be hazardous if exposed to radioactive isotopes like Sm-147, which emits beta radiation. Prolonged exposure may cause skin and eye irritation, as well as lung damage.
  • Environmental effects: The extraction and processing of samarium can cause radioactive contamination and pollution of soil and water if not properly managed.
