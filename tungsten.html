TUNGSTEN

Tungsten is a chemical element. Its symbol on the periodic table is "W".

Tungsten is a metal. It is very hard and heavy. Scientists are studying its properties.

One important property is its very high melting point, meaning it needs a lot of heat to become liquid.

Tungsten is found in the Earth's crust. People extract it from rocks.

Because it is strong and has a high melting point, tungsten is very useful.

It is used in light bulb filaments and to make strong alloys, which are mixtures of metals.

Scientists continue to search for new ways to use the unique properties of this element.
